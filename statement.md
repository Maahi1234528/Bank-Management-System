## The Problem
Currently, many small-scale banks or mock banking systems face the following issues:

Manual Errors: Calculating deposits and withdrawals by hand leads to mistakes in account balances.

Messy Data: It is hard to read long lists of customer data when they are just printed as plain text.

Security Risks: There is no clear boundary between what a "Staff" member can do and what a "Customer" can see.

Lack of Guidance: Basic systems only store money; they do not help the user with financial advice or alerts about their account status.

## The Solution (Our Project)
To solve these problems, this Bank Management System was developed with the following goals:

Automated Calculations: The system handles all math for transactions instantly and accurately.

Professional Tables: Using the Pandas library, all data is organized into neat, readable tables.

Role-Based Menus: Separate access for Staff and Customers ensures that sensitive data (like deleting accounts) stays restricted to staff only.

Smart AI Advisor: We added a logic-based "AI Insight" feature that automatically gives the user financial tips based on how much money they have.

## Conclusion
By using this system, a bank can reduce paperwork, avoid human errors, and provide a much smarter experience to its customers through digital automation.
